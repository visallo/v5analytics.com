Visallo Open-sourced

2015-5-1

V5 Analytics has released Visallo today to the open-source community. Visallo has a track record of being one of the most cutting edge tools in the data-visualization market to date, and V5 Analytics has made the decision to release it to the open-source market to foster adoption, get people with different skill sets to contirbute their talents, and give a business intelligence tool to anyone who wants to pull the code down and run it.

  • If you would like to get started, visit Visallo on Github.

  • If you would like to ask a question, visit the Visallo Google Group

  • If you would like an expert to work with you to integrate Visallo, visit our contact page
